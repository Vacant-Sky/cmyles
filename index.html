Founder's Profile


Subramanian (Subu)

Ceo

Subramanian (Subu) is a senior level professional with over 25 years track record of leading successful “Best-in-class” Human Resources, Learning & Development and Operation teams to triple digit growth in Fortune 100 companies in the Banking, Financial services & Insurance (BFSI), Outsourcing / Offshoring and Hospitality industries. He held leadership positions in GE Capital, Countrywide Financial Services International and MetLife Inc. He has hands on experience in planning and executing strategies for Capability build, Organizational design & development, Talent acquisition, management & development, Change management, Leadership development, Employee engagement, motivation & communication, Diversity & Inclusion practices, Learning & training development and Domain knowledge capability. His professional certifications include ‘Green Belt’ in Six Sigma/LEAN, Situational Leadership II, DiSC profiling, Coaching & Mentoring, Executive coaching, Behavioral event interviewing (BEI), Psychometric evaluation tools, Train the Trainer (TTT) and Instructional Design. Subu is an alumni of the Indian School of Business (ISB), with association to the Kellogg School of Management - Northwestern University and the Wharton School - University of Pennsylvania and cherishes his excellent network of contacts in public & private sector across multiple industries. ￼￼￼
